Members

Being a member of Motorhead has not always been the easiest task for any musician past or present. Take a glance at all the members who have had the duty of playing in the band.

Learn More »

Ian Fraser Kilmister known to the world as Lemmy.

Lemmy joined the space rock band Hawkwind as a bassist and vocalist with no previous experience as a bass guitarist.

In 1984, an audition welcomed in Phil Campbell.

Inspired by guitarists such as Jimi Hendrix, Black Sabbath's Tony Iommi, Led Zeppelin's Jimmy Page, Jan Akkerman, Michael Schenker and Todd Rundgren. Campbell has since toured the world with the band and has remained a constant member of Motörhead, despite a number of changes.

And lastly, drummer extraordinaire Mikkey Dee.

Swedish rock musician, Dee is best known for his time in British rock band Motörhead, with whom he played for from 1992 to 2015.
